Who are we

We are a small group of friend with differnt backgrounds adn a great common interess: Holland football fans and especially "Johan's" fans.

Two of us had the opportunity to see the The Number 14 in field.

We created this website to bring together people around the world to socialize with each other and to have a good time in different locations. We organiye tours and meetings to explore the legacy Johan left behind.

All of us are easy going and open to visit everything that has to do with the greatest football player ever, let it be a city, museum, stadium or bar, it doesn't matter.

We would like not only to bring in memories people who had the possibility to see history magic live, but also to learn to young interested people something about "total football" mentality and Johan's role in it.
